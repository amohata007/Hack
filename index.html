About Event

Smart Business Hackathon is an initiative sponsored by Technical Education Quality Improvement Program by Government of India (TEQIP) and will be jointly organized by Rajasthan Technical University and JECRC Foundation, Jaipur. Smart Business Hackathon is an innovative competition of individuals, young entrepreneurs, assorted into groups of 3-5 participants, in solving business cases.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the registration fee?

For RTU students,it's free & for non rtu students fees is 2500

What about accomodation and food?

Accomodation and food are absolutely free.

Will travel expenses be reimbursed?

Travel expenses will be bare by yourself.

What is the last date of registration?

The last date of registration is 8thJan 2019.

What can we build?

Proper Business idea with financial model,presentation & prototype.

Who can apply?

Everyone is heartly welcome.

What is the criteria for selection of a team?

Evaluation criteria will include novelty of the idea, complexity, clarity and details in the prescribed format.

Can I be a part of more than 1 team?

After registration, participants are required to form their team by logging in.

What should be the size of a team?

A team can consist of 3 to 5 members.
